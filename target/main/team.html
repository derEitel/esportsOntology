Fnatic

Fnatic is a professional electronic sports team headquartered in London. Founded July 23, 2004, Fnatic has a number of esports teams on its roster, including one of the world's best Counter-Strike team. and one of the best League of Legends teams in the World. Fnatic operate a dedicated gaming house for its League of Legends team based in Berlin, Germany. Fnatic also operate an independent digital agency, Sannpa, for businesses looking to be involved with Esports.
